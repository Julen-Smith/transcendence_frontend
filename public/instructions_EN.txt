Pong Game Instructions


Pong is a two-dimensional sports game that simulates table tennis. 

The player controls a paddle located on the left or right side of the screen, by moving it vertically. 

Players use the paddles to hit a ball back and forth.

The goal of the game is to reach five points before the opponent. Points are earned when your opponent fails to return the ball.

The ball starts in the center of the screen and is served to one side.

Controls:

Player 1 (Left Paddle):                      
Move up: Press the "W" key.                  
Move down: Press the "S" key.                

Player 2 (Right Paddle):
Move up: Press the "Up Arrow" key.
Move down: Press the "Down Arrow" key.

Have fun!
